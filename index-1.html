Wiroy Shin

A simple template for job market candidates inspired by inspired by Wiroy Shin's job market site.

Thi template was created by Bruno Salcedo and is distributed uner the attached you-can-do-whatever-you-want-with-it-as-long-as-it's-legal-and-you-agree-that-I'm-not-responsible-if-things-don't-work license.

The information contained in the template is based on EJMR's favorite fictional character BB La Croix.

contents

The site template consists of 4 HTML files corresponding to 4 sections (home,research,cv and contact) and 2 CSS style files: 1. shin.css controls the general structure of the site. 2. style.css controls easy to customize features like fonts, colors and the legent that appears at the bottom of every page. Additionally I include a CV file to illustrate how links should work. Other links to files have been disabled.

instructions

To personalize the template follow four simple steps. 1. Replace BB's info with your own on the HTML files. 2. Create a folder with any documents (cv, images, papers, school logo) and adjust the corresponding links on the HTML files. 3. Adjust any simple style preferences following the structions in style.css. 4. Make any major adjustments to the design on the shin.css file.
